Josh Kennelly

I am a designer and developer. Included in this portfolio are the projects I have worked on, both from school and work.

I studied Software Engineering and Web Development at BYU-Idaho and worked for BYU-Idaho as a Web Developer and Graphic Designer as part of their Online Curriculum Development team.

At work, I was responsible for creating graphic banners and course templates for the classes we were developing for online students.

Our team would meet with the course instructors to identify the needs of their courses. When a concept was too difficult to understand without the guidance of the instructor, we would design infographics and interactive content to help the students learn these concepts.
