360o View of Skull

About the Project

This activity was created for students taking the evolution course. On campus students have access to models of skulls. This activity allows students to get a 360 view of those models.

Because of the work involve to create this, we ended up creating an alternative activity to study the skulls.

Things I learned

  • How to use a green screen to erase backgrounds
  • How to take frames from video footage
  • How to smoothly animate those frames using JavaScript

How It works

Use your mouse or finger to rotate the skull.
