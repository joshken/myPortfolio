Background

I am a designer and developer from Sugar City, Idaho. I studied Software Engineering and Web Development at BYU-Idaho. They hired me as a Web Developer and Graphic Designer for Online Curriculum Development.

At work, I was responsible for creating graphic banners and course templates for our online courses.

A team of us would meet regularly with the instructors to identify the needs of each course. Occasionally, we were asked to design infographics and interactive activities to help online students understand difficult concepts.

I have helped redesign their logo and set up tenant information in our new software, Yardi Voyager.
