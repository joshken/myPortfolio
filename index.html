Background

I am a designer and developer from Sugar City, Idaho. I studied Software Engineering and Web Development at BYU-Idaho and worked for BYU-Idaho as a Web Developer and Graphic Designer as part of their Online Curriculum Development team.

At work, I was responsible for creating graphic banners and course templates for the classes we were developing for online students.

Our team would meet with the course instructors to identify the needs of their courses. When a concept was too difficult to understand without the guidance of the instructor, we would design infographics and interactive content to help the students learn these concepts.

Now

I'm currently working as a Property Manager in the Seattle area. I continue to use my skills in Web Development and Graphic Design to better the company I work for. I have helped redesign their brand and have created tools online to improve their efficiency.

I'm hoping to find a job where I can continue to grow and improve my knowledge of Web Development.
